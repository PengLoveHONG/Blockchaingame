How to Play

Enter a game

Connect to your game session through the game ID and enter your username. A lobby of players mimics a network of miners.

Start mining!

Using the SHA 256 algorithm, the game will generate a hash of the current block. Once a player finds the correct hash, they can send it to the network which will either send it to all the players, 10 players, 5 players, or 2 players. This is to simualate the network and the fragmentation within the blockchain.

Accept or Reject Requests

Players will recieve blockchain requests from other players. Players will then have the choice to accept incoming request blockchains or reject it.

Longest chain wins

Being on the longest blockchain, or the block with the largest block height is the goal of the game, as fragmentation within the blockchain will result in players contributing to shorter chains resulting in no block rewards.
